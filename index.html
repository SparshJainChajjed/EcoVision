How to use EcoVision

  1. Upload: Good lighting; include front & back.
  2. Assess: We highlight visible damage.
  3. Details: Select model & purchase date; flip toggles for internals.
  4. Estimate: See a damage-adjusted value and confidence.
  5. Share location: To surface nearby partner options.
  6. Choose: Paid partners (e.g., Cashify, Zolopik, Attero, ReGlobe, Servify, Karo Sambhav) or give it away yourself.
  7. Suggested ask: If listing yourself, we show what to ask.
  8. Reminder: The value shown is based on your inputs—dealers may vary.
