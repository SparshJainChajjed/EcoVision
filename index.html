Exactly what the app does

• Looks at your uploaded phone photos and assesses damage.
• Asks for model and when you bought it.
• Checks internals (camera, speaker, charging, motherboard…).
• Shows a damage-aware price for your phone.
• Asks if you want to recycle — if yes, asks your location.
• Asks if you want money; if yes, connects you to partners like Cashify or Zolopik based on condition.
• If you give it away yourself, shows credible places.
• Gives a suggested ask price and warns not to lie: it’s only an estimate from your inputs, not a dealer quote.
