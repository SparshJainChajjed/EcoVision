AI that helps you recycle right

About the Idea

eWaste Vision is a mobile-friendly AI web app that uses MobileNetV3 to identify and classify electronic waste — like phones, chargers, and batteries — directly from your camera.

It helps users recycle responsibly by showing disposal guidance and nearby e-waste centers, all while working offline using lightweight AI models.

What It Does

  • Identifies devices such as phones, chargers, and cables
  • Gives recycling or reuse tips
  • Shows nearby e-waste drop-off points
  • Tracks your “green score” for gamified sustainability

How It Works

  1. Your camera captures an image of the item.
  2. The MobileNetV3 model classifies it instantly.
  3. The app gives recycling suggestions or reuse ideas.
  4. You can locate authorized disposal centers near you.

How to Use

  • Open the app and allow camera access.
  • Point your camera at the electronic item.
  • Wait for identification and recycling tips.
  • Follow directions to drop-off centers or earn eco-points.

Why It Matters
