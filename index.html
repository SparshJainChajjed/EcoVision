Exactly what happens

• You tap EcoVision → app opens.
• You upload photos → we assess damage.
• You select model and purchase date.
• You answer Google-Form style questions (power, cameras, charging, speakers, motherboard).
• We show a damage-aware value.
• If you choose to recycle, we ask for your location.
• If you want money, we connect you to partners based on condition; or show places to give away.
• We provide a suggested ask and warn not to lie — it’s not a dealer quote.
